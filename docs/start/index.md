# Getting Started

## What This Section Covers
This section introduces RansNet hardware appliances (aka **mbox**) and softwaare platform (aka **mfusion**), and guides you through the complete lifecycle of deploying and securing a device.

## Why It Matters
Following this workflow ensures that devices are properly deployed, onboarded, and secured for reliable production operation.

## What You Will Learn
- Understanding RansNet products and architecture  
- Preparing and bootstrapping new devices  
- Onboarding devices into mfusion  
- Applying security hardening best practices
