RansNet develops end-to-end SD-WAN/SD-Branch and Wi-Fi Hotspot solutions for enterprise networks and service provider deployments.

### CMG – Cloud Managed Gateway

The CMG series is deployed as an SD-WAN gateway, available as both hardware and virtual appliances.

It integrates key networking and security functions into a single platform, including:

- IP routing  
- Multi-WAN load balancing  
- VPN connectivity  
- Bandwidth control (QoS)  
- Stateful firewall inspection  
- Application filtering  

By consolidating these capabilities into one purpose-built device, CMG reduces infrastructure cost, simplifies deployment, and minimizes potential points of failure.

---

### HSA – HotSpot Access Series

The HSA series is a branch router with integrated 4G cellular connectivity, designed for SD-Branch deployments.

It supports:

- 4G, Machine-to-Machine (M2M) and IoT applications  
- Enterprise networking features  
- Full enterprise networking and security features 

---

### UA – Universal Access Series

The UA series is a branch router with integrated 5G cellular connectivity, designed for high-performance SD-Branch deployments.

It supports:

- 5G connectivity for higher bandwidth and lower latency  
- M2M and IoT use cases  
- Full enterprise networking and security features  

---

### mfusion – SD-WAN Orchestrator

The RansNet mfusion platform is a multi-tenant, customizable central monitoring and management system.

It provides:

- Centralized monitoring of link utization, device status, wireless status, and traffic analysis.
- Fault detection and alerting based on defined thresholds
- Zero-touch provisioning, SD-WAN orchestration and policy management
- Firmware and patch management  

A typical RansNet SD-WAN deployment includes:

- Branch devices (HSA/UA – SD-Branch)  
- Gateway devices (CMG – SD-WAN Gateway)  
- mfusion orchestrator (central management platform)

## Wi-Fi Hotspot Solutions

RansNet Wi-Fi Hotspot solutions consist of the following product families:

- HotSpot Gateway (HSG) Series — [Datasheet](RansNet%20HSG%20Series%20Datasheet.pdf)
- Universal Access Point (UAP) Series — [Datasheet](RansNet%20UAP-520%20Datasheet.pdf)
- HSA and UA Series

---

### HSG – HotSpot Gateway

The HSG series is a captive portal gateway designed to provide secure and controlled guest Internet access.

It enables enterprises and venue operators (such as hotels, malls, F&B outlets, vessels, and campuses) to deliver flexible access policies for guests, visitors, and VIP users.

Key functional modules include:

- Routing, stateful firewall, DHCP server, and Multi-WAN  
- SD-WAN gateway capability  
- Captive Portal (CP) server  
- Authentication, Authorization, and Accounting (RADIUS)  
- Hotspot controller with bandwidth and quota management  

---

### UAP – Universal Access Point

The UAP series is an enterprise-grade wireless access point designed for high-density environments and flexible deployment scenarios.

It supports:

- Indoor and outdoor deployments  
- Mesh networking  
- High client density environments such as malls, offices, schools, hospitality venues, and dormitories  

A typical deployment with HSG and UAP enables on-premise captive portal services.

---

### HSA & UA Series (Wi-Fi Integration)

In addition to SD-WAN capabilities, the HSA and UA series include built-in dual-band Wi-Fi functionality.

They can operate as:

- Router and gateway  
- Wireless mesh controller for UAP devices  
- Direct Wi-Fi access provider for end users  

HSA and UA devices can also integrate with a central HSG (cloud or on-premise) to redirect users to a centralized captive portal for authentication and access control.

Deployments where HSG acts as both the SD-WAN gateway and centralized captive portal server for HSA/UA devices are also supported.
