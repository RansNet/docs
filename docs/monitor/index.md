# Device Monitoring

## What This Does
The Device Monitoring section provides centralized visibility into the health, performance, and status of all managed devices.

## Why It Matters
Administrators can monitor network conditions in real time, detect faults early, and take corrective actions to maintain service availability and performance.

## Where to Access
Navigate to **ORCHESTRATOR → Monitoring** to access all monitoring features.
